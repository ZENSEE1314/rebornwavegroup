KTV Loyalty & Voting App Description for Coder
This mobile app will serve as a loyalty platform, booking system, and a gamified voting community for our KTV venue. It should support user interaction, admin control, and ranked competitions to enhance user engagement and monetization.

🔐 User Functions
1. Authentication
Email, phone, or social login.

Profile photo upload, nickname, gender (optional), birthday.

2. Booking System
View availability by room type and floor.

Book appointments and receive confirmation.

Reschedule/cancel bookings.

View booking history.

3. Loyalty System
Earn points for every purchase (1000 RP = 1 point).

Track total points, current rank, progress bar.

Redeem products/services via points.

4. Purchase History
View past orders, date, total RP, and points earned.

5. News & Promotions
Browse carousel/news list for events and promotions.

Push notification support.

⭐ KOS (King Of Singers) Tab
This tab is a voting-based competition area split into two sections: Tournament and Individual. It displays ranked lists based on stars earned from other users.

🔥 Subtab 1: Tournament
Voting System: Users vote for their favorite singers using Stars (purchased in app: 1 star = 1000 RP).

Timer Countdown: Weekly tournament (7-day countdown).

Reward Distribution (on timer end):

Top 10 users get rewarded in RP based on total stars collected that week:

🥇 1st Place: 50%

🥈 2nd Place: 20%

🥉 3rd Place: 10%

🏅 4th–10th: Remaining 20% shared equally

Stars cannot be claimed immediately – they are distributed only after the countdown ends.

🌟 Subtab 2: Individual
Live Voting & Claiming:

Users get stars in real time and can claim credits immediately.

Ranking Board:

Shows top 1000 users (paginated, 100 per page).

Display profile picture, username, total stars received.

Each user's card shows:

Top 3 contributors (users who sent the most stars)

"Send Star" button

"Like User" button (free like/unlike)

Monetization:

Stars purchased at 1000 RP each

Conversion to credits:

70% to the user

30% platform/admin fee
