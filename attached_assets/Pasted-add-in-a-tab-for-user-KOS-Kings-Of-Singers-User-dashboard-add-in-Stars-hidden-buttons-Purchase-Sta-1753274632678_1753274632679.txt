⭐ KOS (King Of Singers) Tab
This tab is a voting-based competition area split into two sections: Tournament and Individual. It displays ranked lists based on stars earned from other users.

🔥 Subtab 1: Tournament
Voting System: Users vote for their favorite singers using Stars.

Leader Board:

Shows all users (paginated, 100 per page).

Display profile picture, username, total individual stars received.

Each user's card shows:

Top 3 contributors (users who sent the most stars)

"Send Star" button

"Like User" button (free like/unlike)

Timer Countdown: Weekly tournament (7-day countdown).

Reward Distribution (on timer end):

Top 10 users get rewarded total stars collected that week:

🥇 1st Place: 50%

🥈 2nd Place: 20%

🥉 3rd Place: 10%

🏅 4th–10th: Remaining 20% shared equally

Stars cannot be claimed immediately – they are distributed only after the countdown ends.

🌟 Subtab 2: Individual
Live Voting:

Users get stars in real time and can claim credits immediately.

Ranking Board:

Shows all users (paginated, 100 per page).

Display profile picture, username, total individual stars received.

Each user's card shows:

Top 3 contributors (users who sent the most stars)

"Send Star" button

"Like User" button (free like/unlike)

Monetization:

Stars purchased at 1000 RP each

Conversion to credits:

70% to the user

30% platform/admin fee
